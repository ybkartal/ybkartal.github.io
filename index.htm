Yusuf Barış Kartal

About

I am an assistant professor of mathematics at the National University of Singapore. I am interested in symplectic topology, homological mirror symmetry and Floer homotopy theory. Previously, I was a postdoctoral research associate at the University of Edinburgh, supported by Nick Sheridan's grant, and before that a math instructor at Princeton University. I have received my PhD from MIT in June 2019 under the supervision of Paul Seidel.
